#Sharable-Lists

##Abstract

Many people keep a to-do list for their personal benefit, but sometimes it would be nice to share those lists with others. Unfortunately, there aren’t many easy ways to share to-do lists with your friends, family, or coworkers. Our application would allows users to share their to-do lists with other people, so that the whole group can easily keep track of what’s been done and what’s still left to do. A native web application, our product will give users the freedom to manage their to-do lists on any device with a modern web browser.

Planning a housewarming party with your roommates? Going camping with your old college friends? Preparing for a big business presentation with your team? Let us help you stay organized, so you can focus on making your event unforgettable.
